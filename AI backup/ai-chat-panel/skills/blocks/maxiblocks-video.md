# Skill: MaxiBlocks Video Maxi

## Purpose
Video Maxi (`maxi-blocks/video-maxi`) inserts a video with controls or lightbox behavior in MaxiBlocks layouts.【F:src/blocks/video-maxi/block.json†L1-L17】

## Key Controls
- **Block registration:** `maxi-blocks/video-maxi` with the description "Insert a video with controls or lightbox."【F:src/blocks/video-maxi/block.json†L1-L13】

## Common Patterns
- **Demo section:** Video with supporting text and CTA.
- **Hero media:** Video as the focal point of a landing section.

## Accessibility Notes
- Provide captions or transcripts.
- Ensure controls are keyboard accessible.

## Examples
- Product demo video with a short summary.
- Testimonial video in a slider.

## Gotchas / Troubleshooting
- If autoplay is blocked, rely on user-initiated playback controls.
